About Us

Triple A's Kids World is a one-stop event agency dedicated to creating magical experiences tailored to your dream occasions. Our specialty lies in organizing memorable and enchanting kids' events, designed to captivate and delight young hearts. Nestled in the heart of Freetown, Sierra Leone, we pride ourselves on delivering unforgettable moments for every celebration.

We specialize in event decoration, transforming venues into enchanting spaces that bring your vision to life. Our boutique is filled with delightful kids' wear and accessories, offering a wide range of charming outfits and unique items. Additionally, we host a special December show and carnival that ignites the Christmas atmosphere, creating magical memories for the kids during the festive season.

Decorations

At Triple A's Kids World, we transform your events into unforgettable memories. Whether it's a birthday party, a wedding, or any special occasion, our expert decoration services cater to your specific needs and themes. From vibrant balloons to elegant floral arrangements, we ensure every detail is perfect for your celebration.

Boutique

Our boutique offers a wide range of stylish and comfortable kids' clothing and accessories. We carefully select each item to ensure it meets our high standards of quality and fashion. Whether you're looking for casual wear, party outfits, or unique gifts, Triple A's Kids World boutique has something special for every child.

December Program

Every December, Triple A's Kids World hosts a magical program for children, where each child can bring along an adult to share in the festive fun. The event includes gift-giving, food coupons, and various activities designed to create lasting memories. It's our way of spreading joy and giving back to the community during the holiday season.
